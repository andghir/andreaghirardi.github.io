Overview

Approccio

The identity centres on a geometric mark derived from the section of a classical column, reduced to its essential geometry, then refined to feel contemporary. The wordmark uses a custom-drawn serif that bridges the gap between tradition and precision.

A strict two-tone palette, deep architectural blue and warm ivory, anchors the system across print, digital, and environmental applications. Every touchpoint from business cards to site hoardings follows the same strict proportional system.
