Approccio

The magazine's identity is rooted in a rigorously proportioned grid, a six-column system that allows freedom within structure. Feature spreads break the grid deliberately, while regular sections maintain strict order.

Two typefaces carry the entire publication: a high-contrast display serif for headlines and pull quotes, and a workhorse grotesque for body text and captions. The interplay between them gives each section a distinct voice while maintaining a coherent whole.
