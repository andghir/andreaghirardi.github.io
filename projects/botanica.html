Approccio

The system is built around a precise constraint: every label must communicate clearly across containers of different sizes, from a 15ml vial to a 200ml jar. The answer is a strict typographic hierarchy, ingredient origin at the top, product name centred and large, usage notes at the base.

A narrow sans paired with a delicate italic ensures legibility even on the smallest containers. The choice of uncoated stock and flat printing colours reinforces the brand's narrative of honesty and craft.
