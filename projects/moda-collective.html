Approccio

The campaign's visual language is rooted in contrast: hard geometry against soft fabric, stark light against deep shadow. A custom display typeface, with compressed proportions and extreme weight, anchors the campaign across all formats.

For digital, we extended the graphic language into animated formats: kinetic text treatments and slow-motion footage edited in short loops, maintaining the tension of the stills while giving the brand a presence in motion.
